Understand Australian Road Rules

View More

Remember always to drive on the left in Australia. There are a lot of road rules and regulations in Australia that are very strictly enforced. If the drivers go one kilometer over the speed limit and get caught in Australia, the drivers will be fined and lose points from their license. There are fixed cameras and hidden ones everywhere. Their job is to raise revenue while keeping us all safe. There are signs everywhere indicating speed limits, but they change quickly so stay alert. Every driver starts off with zero points, and if they infringe any rule or regulation, they will gain points. Once they hit 13, that is it. No license and a lot more unpleasant things are coming their way. On long weekends or holiday periods, there are double demerit points which mean that if the drivers are caught speeding then instead of getting 2 merit points, they will then get 4. Generally, Australians walk on the left of the footpath and Keeping stand on the left side while riding escalator, leaving the right side for other people to walk.
